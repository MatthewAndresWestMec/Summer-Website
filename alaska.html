June 16th 2023

Embarking on a Norwegian cruise to Alaska was a journey of awe-inspiring beauty and unforgettable moments. As the ship glided through the tranquil waters of the Inside Passage, surrounded by towering mountains and mirrored reflections, a sense of wonder filled the air. Exploring Juneau's glaciers and Skagway's rugged landscapes provided a tangible connection to nature's power and history. The sight of glaciers in Glacier Bay National Park left me humbled by their immensity and the serenity of their surroundings. The weather was actually pretty nice. Sadly, I saw no polar bears.
