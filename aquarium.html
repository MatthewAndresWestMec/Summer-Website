June 28th 2023

Stepping into the Georgia Aquarium in Atlanta was like stepping into a vibrant underwater world teeming with wonder. The colossal Ocean Voyager exhibit, with its expansive viewing window, instantly transported me to the depths of the ocean. Enormous whale sharks gracefully glided by, surrounded by a kaleidoscope of marine life. The ethereal beauty of jellyfish, the playful antics of otters, and the dazzling colors of tropical fish all captured my attention in mesmerizing ways. To be honest, the aquarium was the best part of nationals. The rest was terrible.
